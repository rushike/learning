## Eigen Values and Eigen Vector
For $L: V → V$ is linear and for some scalar λ and $v \neq 0 V$
$$Lv = λv$$

Here.
- λ is an eigenvalue of L 
- v is an eigenvector v of L

### Finding eigenvalues
Solve the equation $det(λI − M) = 0$
